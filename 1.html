Soye Yun / Undergraduate student

Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (KAIST) 

Welcome to my page : )

This website documents my project of exploring questions through methods of humanities text analysis.

What I am curious about is: 'What differences exist in the types of linking words authors primarily use to develop their arguments based on their academic backgrounds (STEM vs. Humanities)?'

For my project to investigate this curiosity, I started with very narrow set of variables. The parameter I focused on is linking words especially along various types of linking words. I believe linking words can not only be function words but also reveal the preferred way to develop the argument.

I felt so much fun with this research!!
